ComputingEd@UW

Welcome to ComputingEd@UW, a community of faculty and students at UW passionate about research and practice on the learning of computing.

Our community spans faculty and students from:

  • The Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering,
  • The Information School,
  • The College of Education, and
  • Human-Centered Design & Engineering.

We'll be posting more details here soon, including who's in our community, ways to join the community, ways to engage in computing education research, and ways to learn the science of teaching and learning computing.
