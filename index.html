ComputingEd@UW

Welcome to ComputingEd@UW, a community of faculty and students at UW passionate about research and reflection on the learning of computing.

Our community spans the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering, The Information School, The College of Education, and Human-Centered Design & Engineering, and students from across campus.

We'll be posting more details here soon, including who's in our community, resources for engaging in research, and resources for learning to teach computing.
